Sightseeing

Nature of the national park Tunkinsky is unique and extremely beautiful. It has high mountains with snowy peaks, dense coniferous forests, fast-flowing rivers with crystal clear water, waterfalls, endless plains, rare plants, curative mineral springs.

To enjoy the beauty of this place, plan out your time for sightseeing. When you will arrive to Arshan village, you must visit the waterfall on Kyngyrga river and Crystal lake located high in the mountains.

First waterfall on Kyngyrga river. Kyngyrga is a mountain river. Coming down from the mountains, the river flows roaring through Arshan village and flows further along the Tunkinsky valley. That is why locals call it the Drum. There are several waterfalls on the river, but the first is the largest. The path to the first waterfall is not hard. It’s length is about 2 km.

There are fences and stairs in the places that are considered dangerous. When you climb up high enough, you’ll see how the river flows in a narrow gorge on the marble bottom. The gorge is called the Marble Gorge. Right before the waterfall, there will be a steep slope. Be careful. When you will go down the slope, you’ll see the waterfall. Cross the bridge over the river and drink the delicious and clear water from the mountain river.

Hiking in the mountains to the Crystal Lake is only done with a guide as the route is hard. For experienced tourists the climb will take 6 hours and 5 hours on the way back with short breaks. During the climb you will pass mixed and coniferous forest, you will walk through alpine meadow and you will finally see goltsy. You have arrived and the Crystal lake is right in front of you. You are 1776,5 meters above sea level. Locals call this lake The Dead Lake because there’s no fish in it. The Artemyev Lake is another name for the Crystal Lake. You are tired, yet happy to have come such a long distance. But now you will also have to go down...

Do not forget about the rules when hiking

  1. You must only go with a guide.
  2. You must not scream in the mountains. Loud noise can cause rockslide.
  3. You must not hike unless completely healthy
  4. You need comfortable clothes and shoes.
  5. You should take potable water, food and matches.
  6. Use mosquito repellents.
  7. Protect your skin and use the sunblock.

The beauty of Tunkinsky valley is breathtaking. Come and see for yourself! You’re always welcome!

Going up this street, you’ll get to the health resort - “Arshan”.

It has a lot of different mineral springs, and there’s also a mongolian market. Next to it there’s a path to the waterfalls and the Peak of Love.
You can cross Traktovaya street and 150 meters away from the guest house there’s another health resort - “Sayany”.
