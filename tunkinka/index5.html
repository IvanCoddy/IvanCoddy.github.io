The Tunkinsky National
Park Rules of Conduct.

It is prohibited to:

  1. dump rubbish on the trails
  2. cut trees and bushes
  3. interfere in the national park wildlife
  4. wonder of the trail
  5. make notes on the rocks and trees, cultural and historical monuments, stands and indicators
  6. shout in the forest, play out loud
  7. bring pets to the trail
  8. come close to the animals, it is dangerous.
  9. go to the trail in a poor physical state
  10. pick up medicinal herbs and flowers

Pay attention to the pictures of endangered plants. It is forbidden to step and pick them up.
